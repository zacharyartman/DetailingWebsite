    • Maintenance Program

    We now offer a maintenance program where we come out and clean your car once a month or every other week. This is available after your car has been detailed by us.

    The goal of our maintenance program is to maintain your vehicle in a great condition on a monthly, weekly, or bi-weekly basis. It includes a thorough wash, cleaning the wheels, tires, door jambs, and shining up the tires. In the interior, you'll get a thorough vacuum, a light wipe down of all the interior panels, and cleaning the glass and mirrors.

    Keep in mind, not much thorough cleaning will be done here. If your car has a lot of stains and blemishes, this is not going to remove those. That would be reserved for our Premium Interior Detailing.

    Our Maintenance Details range from $40 for a coupe/sedan/small SUV to $45 for a large SUV.
    The process begins by cleaning the wheels and degreasing the tires. Then the car is thoroughly rinsed down and receives a foam bath. After that, the vehicle is hand washed from top to bottom using the two bucket method. A spray wax is then applied to boost the protection of the vehicle and it is hand dried with high-quality microfiber towels. After the tires receive a non-sling tire shine, we move onto the inside. All of the glass is cleaned, the seats and floor mats are thoroughly vacuumed, and all surfaces and door jambs are wiped down and protected.
